Work plan on a team project

1- Students are assigned to teams by the client-manager.

2- Each team gets a separate channel in Slack.

3- The team needs to complete the task:

- choose the name of the team;

- select a Team Lead;

- choose a Scrum Master;

- view project materials.

4- Attend the introductory webinar on "Project start. Planning with a mentor."

5- The Scrum Master needs to populate the Trello board and invite all team members to it.

6- The team needs to distribute tasks among all project participants.

7- Everyone works on their task and timely updates information about the task during its execution.

8- The team addresses the Team Lead with technical questions.

9- The Team Lead communicates with the mentor if there are issues that could not be resolved as a team.

10- The team conducts a daily Stand Up lasting 15-20 minutes.

11- Each tester on Stand Up answers three questions: What did he do yesterday (today)? What will be done today (tomorrow)? What do you need help with?

!!!Importantly!!! at Stand Up, we don't start analyzing the moments where help is needed, we talk about them so that the whole team understands how the process of completing the task goes. After the Stand Up, a tester who needs help can stay and discuss their questions with a team member who knows the answer or with the Team Lead.
